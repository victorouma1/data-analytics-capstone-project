# Europe Cost of Living and Salary Analyser
### Problem statement
In an increasingly globalised world individuals are more open to relocating for better financial opportunities. However higher salaries do not always translate into better living standards or savings due to variations in cost of living ,rent and local economic conditions. There is a need for a data driven solution that helps individuals compare cities and countries based on real purchasing power and potential savings rather than salary alone.
### Desription
This project aims to build an end-to-end data analytics solution that analyzes and compares cost of living and salary data across European countries.

### Research Questions
How does cost of living vary across countries? <br>
What is the trend in cost of living and salary over time? <br>
What are the five most and least expensive cities in Europe? <br>
How is monthly income spent in different European countries? <br>

### Tools
Data cleaning in Power Query <br>
Visualizations with Matplotlib & Seaborn <br>
Interactive maps using GeoPandas <br>
Streamlit<br>
